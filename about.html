Our Story

From humble beginnings to global recognition

The Beginning

Founded in 2020 with a vision to redefine beauty pageants, Pageant Empress was born from the belief that true beauty comes from within. Our founder, inspired by the lack of platforms celebrating intelligence alongside beauty, created a competition that would change the landscape forever.

Our Evolution

What started as a local competition has grown into an international phenomenon. We've expanded across continents, bringing together women from diverse backgrounds, cultures, and experiences. Each year, we've raised the bar higher, adding new categories and initiatives.

Today & Tomorrow

Today, Pageant Empress stands as more than just a beauty pageant. We're a movement, a platform for change, and a beacon of hope for young women worldwide. Our commitment to empowerment, education, and social impact continues to drive us forward.
